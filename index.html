01 — Context

We understand what
you are building.

FarmLink Capital is not just a fintech platform. It is a trust infrastructure for South African agriculture. Your Choose-Optimise-Recommend-Build-Connect process is, at its core, a service design framework. The challenge is making that experience consistent, intuitive, and scalable across every farmer and every financial institution that touches your platform.

We have seen this challenge before. Getting farmers to trust digital systems enough to act on them is not a technology problem. It is a design and experience problem. That is exactly where Constellation works.

Two audiences, one platform

Farmers and enterprise clients have fundamentally different jobs to be done. The experience needs to serve both without compromising either.

Trust before data

Farmers are being asked to share sensitive operational data. Without the right UX signals, trust is never established and adoption stalls before it starts.

Speed to market

The MVP needs to be clean, functional, and enterprise-grade without a 12-month build cycle. Component standardisation and design system thinking accelerate this significantly.

Conversion patterns

The current Figma prototype has strong visual ambition but non-standard conversion patterns. Fixing these early prevents costly rework post-launch.

05 — Terms

Simple, transparent,
no surprises.

Retainers work because both parties know exactly what they are committing to. These are the terms that govern our engagement.

Minimum commitment

3-month minimum on all tiers. Monthly rolling thereafter. Either party may exit with 30 days written notice after the initial term.

Hours policy

Unused hours do not roll over. We ensure every hour is pointed at something that matters. If a task looks like it will run over, we flag it before it happens.

Scope changes

Any work outside the agreed monthly scope is added to the following month's backlog and confirmed before any work begins. No surprises, no unilateral decisions.

Pricing review

Pricing is reviewed at the 3-month mark and annually thereafter. Any changes are communicated with 30 days notice.

VAT

All pricing is exclusive of VAT. VAT will be added to invoices at the applicable rate.

Invoicing

Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month, payable within 7 business days. A formal service agreement will be provided upon acceptance.
